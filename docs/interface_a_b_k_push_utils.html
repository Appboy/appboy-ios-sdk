+ (BOOL) isAppboyInternalRemoteNotification: (NSDictionary *)  userInfo
Parameters
userInfoThe userInfo dictionary passed to application:didReceiveRemoteNotification:fetchCompletionHandler: or application:didReceiveRemoteNotification:.
Returns
YES if the push notification was sent by Appboy for an internal feature.

Appboy uses content-available silent notifications for internal features. You can use this method to ensure your app doesn't take any undesired or unnecessary actions upon receiving Appboy's internal content-available notifications (e.g., pinging your server for content).
